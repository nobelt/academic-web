Nobel Truong

M.S. CS graduate student at UT Austin. Previously at NASA Ames and Micron.

Nobel Truong is a researcher working on formal verification and automated reasoning. Previously, Truong worked on developing methods in prognostics and decision making under uncertainty for lunar rovers at NASA Ames. Prior to this, Truong researched smart manufacturing technologies at Micron Technology and verification of Bayesian network classifiers at UCLA. Truong received her Bachelor of Arts in philosophy from UC Berkeley. Her undergraduate thesis was on employing causal structures as a formal framework for defining empathy.

Truong's slides on computer science and other related topics can be found here. In a second life, Truong also maintains a small industrial design practice.
