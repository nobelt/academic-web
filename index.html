Nobel Truong

Currently at the University of California, Los Angeles. Previously at the University of California, Berkeley.

Nobel Truong is a researcher in computer science at UCLA currently advised by Adnan Darwiche. Her interests lie in knowledge representation, knowledge compilation, causal structures, and causal discovery. Truong received her Bachelor's degree in philosophy from UC Berkeley where she was advised by John Campbell. Her undergraduate thesis was on causal structures and empathy.
