Nobel Truong is an AI researcher currently interning at NASA Ames Research Center, Intellegent Systems Division and Micron Technology, Micron Ventures - AI. Her interests lie in knowledge representation, knowledge compilation, causal structures, and causal discovery. More broadly, she is researching methods in formal hardware and software verification and automated reasoning. Previously, Truong worked on diagnosing behavioral inconsistencies in Bayesian network classifers at UCLA under the supervision of Adnan Darwiche. Truong received her Bachelor's degree in philosophy from UC Berkeley where she was advised by John Campbell. Her undergraduate thesis was on employing causal structures as a formal framework for defining empathy.
